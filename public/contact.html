Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Bina Masjid Digital cost?

Bina Masjid Digital is available for free for basic features. We also offer premium plans with additional features starting at $29/month.

Can multiple people manage a mosque account?

Currently, each mosque has one primary account for security reasons. However, we are working on adding multi-user access in our premium plans.

Is my mosque's financial data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect all mosque data, with special attention to financial information.

How do I export financial reports?

You can export financial reports as PDF by selecting a date range in the Financial Records section and clicking the "Export to PDF" button.
