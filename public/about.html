About Bina Masjid Digital

Learn about our mission to digitize mosque management across the world

Our Story

Bina Masjid Digital was founded in 2023 by a group of Muslim tech professionals who saw the need for better management tools for mosques.

We noticed that many mosques still relied on paper-based systems or disjointed digital tools to manage their announcements, finances, and community engagement. This led to inefficiencies and missed opportunities for growth.

Our mission is to provide mosques worldwide with accessible, easy-to-use digital tools that streamline administrative tasks and increase transparency, allowing mosque leadership to focus more on spiritual guidance and community building.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every mosque has access to affordable, high-quality digital management tools.

We believe that by simplifying administrative tasks and improving transparency, mosques can better serve their communities and focus on their core spiritual mission.

Our goal is to help mosques embrace the digital age while maintaining their traditional values and practices.

Our Core Values

Transparency

We believe in complete financial transparency for mosques to build trust with their communities.

Simplicity

Our tools are designed to be easy to use, requiring minimal technical knowledge.

Security

We prioritize the security of mosque data and implement strong protection measures.

Community

We believe in strengthening mosque communities through better communication tools.
